The Best Restaurant

Established in 2022, Tasty restaurant is Johannesburg's only authentic Social Caffe and cuisine hangout, serving delicious burgers, pizzas and sea food, a flavoursome menu and a lifestyle experience perfect for any for day of the week.

Tasty restaurant brings together family and friends, treating their guests to a fine dine and exquisite cuisine. With its classy and elegant decor to set a warm atmoshpere and chefs with a passion for food, any occasion transforms into a special event.

Culture

Tasty restaurant embraces its Social lifestyle class which is evident in its store. Its culture is embedded in their extensive offering, from the cigars to the delicious menu, the tasty cocktails and premium drinks selection, to the beautifully designed interiors. Tasty is truly unique and a market leader in its industry. The Franchise has pioneered a movement which offers customers a true lifestyle experience in social dining and their appeal to “Come See And Be Seen” has been a driving force in the business’ success.
